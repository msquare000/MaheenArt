the artist

Hello, I'm Maheen

I paint the moment before a forest opens into light — the last bend of a path, the first hush of a night tide, the mist a river keeps from the mountains it left behind. Every canvas begins outdoors, in memory, or in a photograph I couldn't stop thinking about.

Working almost entirely in oil, I build each piece in slow layers — letting the light in last, the way it actually arrives in a forest: thin, golden, and a little unexpected.

“I'm not painting the tree. I'm painting the light that got through it.”
